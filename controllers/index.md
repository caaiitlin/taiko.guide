Choosing a controller can be one of the biggest decisions for home players.

This section compares official and third-party controllers, explains their differences, and provides detailed information to help you choose the controller that's right for you.

Over time, this aims to become one of the most comprehensive English-language resources for Taiko no Tatsujin controllers.
