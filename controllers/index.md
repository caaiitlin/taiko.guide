# Controllers

A good drum controller can make playing Taiko no Tatsujin at home far more enjoyable. From compact entry-level options to full-sized arcade-style controllers, there are controllers available for almost every budget and play style.

This section explains the different types of controllers, how they compare, what to look for before buying, and how to choose the option that best suits your needs.
